About Us

Professional, Innovative, Reliable Construction Partner

HT Construction Ltd was established in 2004, offering comprehensive construction services including architectural design, construction, and project management. We hold top-tier construction certifications and specialize in commercial complexes, residential communities, and public buildings across Auckland and New Zealand.

Our company operates on the principle of "Building Quality for the Future", consistently embracing technological and management innovation. Utilizing advanced BIM technology and green building concepts, we provide comprehensive construction solutions for our clients. Our projects span across New Zealand, with over 500 completed projects covering more than 2 million square meters.
